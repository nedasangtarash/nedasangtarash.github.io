Exploring memory, the body, and childhood misunderstandings through experimental animation and moving images.

Neda Sangtarash (b. 2002, Isfahan) is an animator and experimental artist whose work explores memory, the body, and childhood misunderstandings through moving images.

She began her artistic practice with storyboard, background and character design, as well as pixel art, and later studied Handicrafts, where she developed an interdisciplinary approach to animation.

In her practice, animation becomes a space for making the invisible tangible.
